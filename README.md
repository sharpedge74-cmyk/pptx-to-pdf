# PPTX to PDF Converter – Fast, Secure & Browser-Based



Convert your PowerPoint (PPTX/PPT) files into high-quality **PDF** instantly in your browser. No software installation, no sign-ups, and no uploads to a server!

---

## Key Features

- ✅ **Upload PPT or PPTX files** – Convert slides directly from your device.  
- ✅ **Instant preview** – Check your slides and order before converting.  
- ✅ **One-click conversion** – Generate a PDF quickly while preserving formatting.  
- ✅ **Download-ready PDF** – All slides included, properly sized and high-quality.  
- ✅ **Browser-based & private** – Files stay on your device; nothing is uploaded.  
- ✅ **Thumbnail navigation** – Browse slides easily in the preview before exporting.  

---

## How It Works

1. Upload your PPTX or PPT file.  
2. Preview your slides using the built-in viewer.  
3. Click **Convert** to generate the PDF.  
4. Download the PDF instantly and share or archive.
